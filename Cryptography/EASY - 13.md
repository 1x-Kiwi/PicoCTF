# How to solve the '13' CTF.

## Description
- "Cryptography can be easy, do you know what ROT13 is?"
## Hints
- This can be solved online if you don't want to do it by hand!

## Solution
- Copy the garbled text that is in the description
- Go to https://rot13.com
- Paste the garbled text into the top box (DO NOT CHANGE THE MIDDLE DROP DOWN OFF OF 'ROT13')
- Copy your new found flag.
- Go back to PicoCTF past your flag in the box and click submit!
