# How to solve
- Download the image that is provided in the description called 'numbers.png'
- Open the '.png' file in your software of choice.
- When you open the file you will be greeted with a collection of numbers and a very colourful background. Do not be thrown off with the colourful background it means nothing your main focus is the numbers themselves.
- The numbers represent a specific letter in the alphabet refer to the table below.

| 1   | 2   | 3   | 4   | 5   | ... |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| A   | B   | C   | D   | E   | ... |
- To solve the CTF you will connect each number in the image with it't matching letter in the alphabet.
- You can either type your answer directly in the answer box on PicoCTF or a text document.
- Once you have finished either click submit or paste your flag and click submit!

# How to reconcile this type of 'encryption'
A very simple way to recognize this type of encryption is to simply look at the numbers. If the range is between 1-26 you can safely assume it is using this method.
